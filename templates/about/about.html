About This Project

The IoT-based Accident Detection System is designed to monitor real-time vehicle conditions using sensors such as:

  • ADXL345: Detects impact in X, Y, Z axes.
  • Ultrasonic Sensor: Measures obstacles or sudden distance changes.
  • Vibration Sensor: Detects shocks or sudden movements.

When an accident is detected, the system records the data, sends it to a central server, and alerts predefined recipients via email. This system helps in rapid response to accidents and ensures vehicle safety monitoring.

Features

Real-time Monitoring

Continuously monitors impact, vibration, and distance in vehicles.

Accident Alerts

Sends instant notifications to emergency contacts when an accident occurs.

Data Analytics

Stores sensor readings for analysis, visualization, and reporting.
